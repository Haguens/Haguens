Andrés Manuel López Obrador AKA. AMLO

The best president of Mexico City.

Here's a time line of AMLO's life:

  • 1953 - Born in Tabasco, Mexico
  • 1973 - He entered National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM)
  • 1977 - López Obrador began his career as the director of the Instituto Indigenista in Tabasco.
  • 1983 - Elected president of the state executive committee of the Institutional Revolutionary Party (PRI).
  • 1988 - Led to the formation of the National Democratic Front (FDN).
  • 1989 - FDN consolidated to form the Party of the Democratic Revolution (PRD) with López Obrador becoming the party president in the state of Tabasco.
  • 1996 - López Obrador was elected to the post of the President of PRD, occupying the position from August 2, 1996 to April 10, 1999.
  • 2012 - López Obrador was once again nominated by PRD as their presidential candidate against PRI’s Enrique Peña Nieto and PAN’s Josefina Vázquez Mota, finishing second in the race, acquiring 31.64% vote.
  • 2012 - He created ‘Movimiento Regeneración Nacional (MORENA; the National Regeneration Movement) as a civil association, registering it as a national party with the National Electoral Institute on July 9, 2014.
  • 2018 - López Obrador took office on 1 December
